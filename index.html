Welcome to SRDS 2020

Welcome to the official website of the 38th International Symposium on Reliable Distributed Systems (SRDS 2020). SRDS 2020 is Technically Co-Sponsored by IEEE Computer Society. The conference will be held in Shanghai, China during Oct.1-4 2020.

The conference will serve as an international forum for the presentation and exchange of technological advances and research results in the broad fields of distributed systems design, development and evaluation. The conference will bring together leading researchers, engineers and scientists in the domain of interest from around the world.

We warmly welcome prospective authors to submit your research papers to SRDS 2020, and share your latest research results and valuable experiences with other top-scientists, engineers and scholars from all over the world.
