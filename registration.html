Registration

Registration Options and Fees

All the accepted have been uploaded to be published as part of the proceedings that will be distributed before the scheduled conference (September 21st-24th, 2020). The online publication will include both papers and static presentations for each paper.

The registration fee for each paper is $200. Besides, up to 2 additional pages may be purchased per paper for an additional fee of US$100 per page.

Author/paper fees must be received by September 20th, 23:59 AOE to be published in the proceedings.

Refund Policy

No registrations are refundable.

Important Author Information

For each paper, at least one author must be registered for the paper to be included in the proceedings. The author/paper registration deadline is September 15th, 2020.

Each Author Registration is valid for ONE paper.
